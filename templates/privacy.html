Privacy Policy - RozRashi

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RozRashi ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

RozRashi does not require user registration, login, or account creation. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The app fetches daily horoscope and status content from our server. No personal user data is transmitted during this process.

2. Location Data (Panchang Feature)

RozRashi may request access to your approximate location (city-level) solely to provide accurate daily Panchang (Hindu calendar) information relevant to your region.

  • Only approximate location (coarse) is used — not precise GPS coordinates.
  • Your location is not stored on our servers at any point.
  • Your location is not shared with any third party other than the Panchang calculation service, and only for the purpose of computing today's Panchang.
  • Location data is used in real-time and immediately discarded after the Panchang result is returned.
  • If you decline location permission, the app will automatically use a default location (Ujjain, India) for Panchang calculations.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

RozRashi displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

4. Image Generation Feature

RozRashi allows users to create shareable image cards from status content. This image is generated entirely on your device and is not uploaded to or stored on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

RozRashi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

We do not store any personal user data on our servers. Content displayed in the app (horoscopes, statuses) is publicly served and contains no personal information.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (for advertisements)
  • FreeastrologyAPI (for Panchang calculations — approximate location is sent for computation only)
  • Prokerala (for Kundali / birth chart calculations — birth details entered by the user are sent for computation only and are not stored)

8. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
